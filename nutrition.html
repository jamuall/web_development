The recommended daily intake of essential nutrients for different age groups

Nutrient Children (4-8 years) Teenagers (14-18 years) Adults (19-50 years) Older Adults (51+ years)
Calcium (mg/day) 1,000 1,300 1,000 1,200
Iron (mg/day) 10 11 8 8
Vitamin C (mg/day) 25 65-75 75-90 75-90
Vitamin D (IU/day) 600 600 600 800-1,000
Vitamin E (mg/day) 7 15 15 15

Note: These recommendations are based on the Dietary Reference Intakes (DRIs) developed by the Institute of Medicine of the National Academies in the United States. It's important to keep in mind that individual nutrient needs may vary based on factors such as sex, physical activity level, and overall health status.
